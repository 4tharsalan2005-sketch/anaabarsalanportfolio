About Me
Designer with purpose
I am Anaab Arsalan, a creative graphic designer from Sri Lanka passionate about visual storytelling — especially in brand, commercial, and educational contexts.

I specialize in creating clean, modern, and meaningful visuals. With experience in Canva, Adobe Photoshop, and Adobe Illustrator, I design content that connects with audiences and communicates with clarity and emotion.

Whether it's a full brand identity, a commercial ad, or a business card — every project I take on is driven by purpose and audience connection.
